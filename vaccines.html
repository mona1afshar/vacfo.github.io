mRNA Vaccine Info

The vaccines are given in the upper arm muscle. The mRNA is recieved by immune cells, which use them to make proteins. When the protein is made, the original mRNA is broken down and destroyed. The protein is displayed on the cell's surface, which results in the immune system building antibodies against it
